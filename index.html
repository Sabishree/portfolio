About

A little about me

I'm a frontend developer with a strong interest in building clean, accessible and responsive websites. I enjoy the process of turning a design or an idea into working code, and I pay close attention to small details like spacing, readability and how a page behaves on different screen sizes.

As a fresher, most of my learning has come from building real projects rather than following tutorials alone. I've worked on websites for different use cases — from a safety platform to a hostel booking site — to practise structuring layouts, writing maintainable CSS, and adding interactivity with JavaScript.

I'm currently looking for an opportunity where I can keep learning, contribute to real products, and grow as part of a development team.
